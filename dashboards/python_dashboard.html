Core Business Problem

A major direct-to-consumer e-commerce brand discovered that despite high traffic and initial signups, customer lifetime value (LTV) remained stagnant. Executive leadership was unable to determine whether the revenue flatline was due to customer attrition, poor secondary conversions, or a lack of engagement among high-value customer segments.

This portfolio project addresses this visibility gap by transforming raw transactional records into a robust customer cohort framework and SQL-calculated Recency-Frequency-Monetary (RFM) segmentation. The results enable lifecycle marketing teams to design hyper-targeted campaigns that prevent churn before it occurs.

Immediate Marketing Interventions

  • VIP Reactivation Loop: Our At-Risk segment (16.9% of customers) accounts for $64,000 in historic revenue but hasn't ordered in 90+ days. Automatically deploy a tiered incentive campaign (e.g. $15 off next $50 spent) tied to their past favorite product categories.
  • The Month-1 Onboarding Playbook: Our cohort analysis reveals a massive 54% drop-off in activity from Month 0 to Month 1. Set up automated transactional email prompts 14 days after purchase offering helpful tips, FAQs, and a 10% coupon for accessories.
