# Power BI Dashboard Specification

## Page 1: RFM Customer Segmentation

- KPI cards: customers segmented, total revenue, average monetary value, champions count.
- Bar chart: customer count by segment.
- Scatter plot: recency vs monetary value, colored by segment.
- Table: CustomerID, RFM scores, RFM class, segment.

## Page 2: Cohort Retention Heatmap

- Matrix: cohort month by month index.
- Conditional formatting: darker values show stronger retention.
- KPI cards: month 1 retention, month 3 retention, average repeat activity.
